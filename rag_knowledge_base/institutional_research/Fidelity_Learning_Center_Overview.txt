HOW TO BUILD A PORTFOLIO WITH ETFs (Fidelity's 5-step framework):
1. Ask yourself 3 questions: What's my time horizon? Can I handle market swings? Do I have a steady job, savings, and low debt?
2. Match your mix to your risk:
   - Aggressive portfolio: ~90% stock-focused ETFs, ~10% bond-focused ETFs
   - Cautious portfolio: Balanced mix, e.g., 50% stocks / 50% bonds
3. Diversify across the board:
   - Domestic ETFs: mix of company sizes and industries
   - International ETFs: both developed and emerging markets
4. Use ETF screeners to filter by sector, size, performance
5. Start investing — select the ETF and enter your target allocation
